About Me

I am an American journalist and foreign policy analyst based in Ukraine. I have written for leading publications including The Free Press, The Atlantic Council, The Wall Street Journal, The Kyiv Independent, and National Review. I am the author of the Substack Pulse. I am also a regular on-air commentator on networks like France 24 and CNN, providing analysis on the war.
