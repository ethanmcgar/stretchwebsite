About Me

I am an American journalist and foreign policy analyst based in Ukraine. I have written for leading publications including The Free Press, The Atlantic Council, The Wall Street Journal, The Kyiv Independent, and National Review. I am the author of the Substack Pulse. I also provide on-air commentatary on the war for major news networks including France 24 and CNN.
